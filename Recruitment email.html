Hello Potential Candidate!

Are you a Business, Economics or Hospitality major wanting to develop your professional skills and gain an incredible network of like-minded individuals?

San Francisco State University chapter of Delta Sigma Pi would like to invite you to attend our 2015 Fall Recruitment events! Delta Sigma Pi is a professional co-ed fraternity that prepares business students for successful professional careers and also focuses on community service, academics, and brotherhood.

Delta Sigma Pi has over 260,000 members support the goals and ideals of the Fraternity. Our chapter at San Francisco State University is the oldest professional fraternity on campus and has an extensive network of alumni working at Google, Morgan Stanley, Merrill Lynch, KPMG, Genesys, Fisher Investments, and many more. You will have the chance to meet some of those alumni at our Alumni Panels.

If you would like to get more involved on campus and expand your professional network, join us in our upcoming recruitment events for these 2 weeks. We are so excited to meet you at our events! If you have any questions, contact Cemre Yolveren at svp.dsp@gmail.com.

For more information you can visit our website at www.dspsfsu.com .
